What is TigerHacks?

TigerHacks is a DC-based hackathon run by students at Jackson-Reed High School (Formerly Woodrow Wilson High School). Students will compete for prizes, along with gaining experience from industry partners and mentors.

FAQ

How does it work?

Students will create a project under a *super-secret* theme introduced at the beginning of the project!

Isn't hacking illegal?

Think of our hackathon as more of a coding competition. Hacking can be illegal, but our hackathon is a collaborative engineering competition intended to encourage creativity.

Does it cost money?

Students can attend the hackathon 100% free!

I'm a beginner, can I still join?

Yes! Our hackathon is very beginner-friendly, a nd we will have mentors and teachers available to help you learn new concepts, languages, etc.
